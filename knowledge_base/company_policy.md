# Company Policy Document

## Refund Policy
- Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of their initial purchase.
- Annual subscriptions canceled halfway will not receive prorated refunds, unless approved by a supervisor.
- All refund requests must be reviewed by the Billing department and escalated for supervisor approval.

## Account Closure Policy
- Account closure requests will result in the permanent deletion of user data after a 14-day grace period.
- Account closure must be approved by a supervisor to ensure no outstanding balances or active disputes exist.

## Compensation Policy
- In the event of an outage lasting more than 4 hours, customers may be eligible for service credit.
- Direct financial compensation for data loss or outages requires management escalation and supervisor approval.

## Escalation Policy
- Support agents must escalate critical issues, security breaches, or abusive customer behavior to a supervisor immediately.
