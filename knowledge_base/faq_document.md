# FAQ Document

## Account Management
**Q: I forgot my account password. How can I reset it?**
A: To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. An email with a reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

**Q: How do I activate or deactivate a team member's account?**
A: Go to Team Management in the dashboard. Select the user and toggle the "Active" status. Deactivated users cannot log in but their historical data is retained.

**Q: Can I update my profile information?**
A: Yes, you can update your name, email, and notification preferences by navigating to your Profile Settings.

## General
**Q: How do I contact support if I have a critical issue?**
A: Professional and Enterprise users can use the priority support widget in the app. Enterprise users can also call their dedicated 24/7 support line.

**Q: Do you offer a free trial?**
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan. No credit card is required to sign up.
