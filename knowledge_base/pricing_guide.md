# Pricing Guide

## Subscription Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $19/month or $190/year.
- **Features:** Single user, basic reporting, standard support (response within 24 hours), 10GB storage.

### Professional Plan
- **Price:** $49/month or $490/year.
- **Features:** Up to 5 users, advanced analytics, priority support (response within 4 hours), 100GB storage.

### Enterprise Plan
- **Price:** Custom pricing (contact sales).
- **Features:** Unlimited users, dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone support, unlimited storage, custom integrations.

## Add-ons
- **Extra Storage:** $5/month per additional 50GB.
- **API Access:** Included in Professional and Enterprise plans. Basic plan users can add it for $10/month.

## Payment Terms
- We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise customers.
- Invoices are generated on the 1st of every month.
